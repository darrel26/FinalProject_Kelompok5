Interno

Interno is a interior design website with a delightful and captivating experience. A creative website that highly suitable for interior design, architectural, electrical service, plumbing, painting companies.

**Case Study: Transitioning an Offline Interior Design Store to the Online Realm** **Background:** Sarah, the owner of a successful local interior design store, faced challenges as she saw the potential to expand her reach and clientele by moving her business online. She decided to explore this transition and sought a design thinking approach to ensure a seamless shift to the digital realm. **Design Thinking Approach:** **1. Empathize (Understanding the Challenge):** - Sarah initiated the process by empathizing with her current and potential customers. She conducted surveys, interviews, and gathered feedback to understand their preferences, needs, and concerns related to online interior design services. **2. Define (Problem Definition):** - After gathering insights, Sarah defined the challenge: "Transition the existing offline interior design store into an effective and user-friendly online platform to cater to a wider audience while maintaining the quality of service and customer engagement." **3. Ideate (Generating Ideas):** - Sarah organized brainstorming sessions with her team to generate creative ideas for the online transition. Ideas included creating a user-friendly website, offering virtual consultations, and developing an interactive online catalog. **4. Prototype (Design Prototypes):** - Several design prototypes were developed, including wireframes for the website, mock-ups of virtual consultation processes, and sample online catalogs showcasing available interior design products. **5. Test (Feedback and Iteration):** - The prototypes were presented to a select group of customers and focus groups. They provided valuable feedback on the user experience, navigation, and the overall online shopping process. - Based on the feedback, adjustments were made to improve the online platform's usability and functionality. **Implementation:** **6. Implement (Turning Ideas into Reality):** - With the final design approved, Sarah and her team began the implementation phase.
